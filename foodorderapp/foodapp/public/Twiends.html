Hi, thank you for starting a new subscription with us.


You probably have some questions about how our subscriptions work, so here are the basics:


Recurs weekly, cancel any time
We'll automatically bill you once a week, but you can cancel at any time if you don't want to continue with the trial. Every time your subscription renews we will add 1500 featured displays to your account. You can easily make changes to your plan here:
https://twiends.com/featured


Featured displays are used daily
Your displays are used daily to promote your twitter profile. We will automatically spread these out over the week if you use the auto limit setting on the featured page listed above.


Change to monthly for better performance
We usually start you off on a weekly plan because it is a more flexible option for new users. Monthly plans are more efficient if you want to promote yourself for more than just a few weeks, so let us know if we can move you onto one of these more efficient plans. You'll get more bang for your buck!


Subscriptions can be paused
We know that sometimes you just want to take a break, so we allow you to pause a subscription for up to 3 months. This allows you to stop being billed for a period, while still keeping all other aspects of your promotion active. Please note that subscriptions will automatically resume billing at the end of the paused period, so cancel instead if you are unsure about wanting to continue in the future.


Please feel free to contact us here any time if you need any assistance..!
